NHIF Frequently Asked Questions

By Cecilia Karanja (@ModernMomHq)

I've been getting so many questions in the comments that I decided to compile the most frequently asked, get the answer from NHIF staff and list them here. You can also contact NHIF with your queries by calling these toll free line... 0800 720 601, (020) 2723255/56, (020) 2714793/94, (020) 2722527. Kindly go through the list to see if your question has been answered below before leaving a comment.


How long after registration can you access the NHIF services?

For the informal sector, you have to wait for 60 days while for the formal sector you only wait for 30 days.

How long does it take for a dependant to access services after being added to the plan?

Immediately

How long do Mpesa Payments take to feature in your statement?

Between 24 and 48 hrs, but some payments are even instant.

What happens if you have not paid for long and you need to resume?

You can either pay the backlog and the penalties, or you can just pay the principal amount and wait for 60 days to access the services.

What do you do if you cannot remember your NHIF number?

Just make a call to NHIF using one of the contact numbers given above and you'll be sorted. The numbers are toll free. You can also get your number by texting ID **** to 21101 where **** is your ID number and the message you receive will indicate your NHIF membership number.

How do you go about changing your preferred hospital?

You can only do this twice a year. What you do is wait for a call for registration for the preffered hospital and visit an NHIF branch to do this. If you have a very solid reason as to why you need to change before a call is made, you can always visit your nearest NHIF branch.

When is the deadline for payments?

It is on the 9th of every month.

If I miss the deadline, what are the payments?

If your employer misses the deadline, he pays twice the principal. For the informal contributors, you pay the principal plus 50% of the principle. In other words, if you are to pay on, let's say 12th, instead of paying the normal Ksh. 500 you pay Ksh.750.

How do I get my NHIF Statement Online?

This online feature and others are set to be launched in the next few weeks. Keep it here and we'll keep you posted.

Where do I get additional NHIF information?

Just visit their website at http://www.nhif.or.ke and you'll be able to get all the latest and some more. or For more information; - dial USSD code *155#, - visit: www.nhif.or.ke I Call Toll Free: 0800 720601, - Download the NHIF mobile app “My NHIF” on Apple Store and Google Play Store.

How much will NHIF pay for me when I receive treatment?

This depends on the hospital you choose to visit. There are three categories of NHIF Accredited Hospitals and each category has a different contract with NHIF. Visit the linked post to see which category your hospital falls under and how much NHIF will pay for you.

How can i know the Hospitals accredited by NHIF?

You can get the list of accredited hospitals per county by visiting the NHIF website (http://www.nhif.or.ke/healthinsurance/outpatientServices) or the nearest Branch office or Huduma centre. If you do not see a facility in the list, it means it is not NHIF accredited.

What is the NHIF Supa Cover?

NHIF SUPA Cover is Kenya’s largest, affordable, accessible, sustainable, and quality medical insurance cover that enables you and your family to enjoy an unparalleled benefit package. The Fund covers members in the formal and informal sectors, and those under the Health Insurance Subsidy Programme(HISP) for Elderly & Persons Living with Severe Disabilities, Orphans & Vulnerable children, among other sponsored programmes; all these to ensure no one is left behind.

What if I need treatment outside Kenya?

Foreign Treatment package comes into play when medical and surgical intervention that is not available in the country becomes necessary. It requires approval by Director General of Health and NHIF.

Does NHIF cover Road Ambulance costs?

Yes, it covers emergency evacuations and transportation to the nearest contracted healthcare facility.

I have been trying to select my hospital of choice, but I have not been successful. Please help?

Kindly select your choice of hospital/facility using your feature phone by dialing *155#, the Mobile App “My NHIF” that is available on Play Store/Apple Store and/ or, self-care portal on our website http://www.nhif.or.ke/healthinsurance/nhifselfcare/
